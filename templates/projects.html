Seo-Traker: Monitor Your Keyword Rankings

Seo-Traker is a user-friendly desktop application designed to simplify keyword rank tracking for SEO agencies. With a connection to a server, Seo-Traker verifies your subscription and empowers you to analyze your Google keyword positions.
